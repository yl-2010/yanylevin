About

I like hard problems.

I'm a junior at Eastside Preparatory School in Kirkland, WA. Most of my time goes into competitive math, software research with Professor Milos Gligoric's group at UT Austin, debate, and teaching younger students the kind of math that goes beyond worksheets.

Across those worlds I keep coming back to the same question: how do you make complex ideas clear enough to share, whether that's a proof, a research artifact, or a product for recruiters?
